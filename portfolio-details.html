  • Title: Custom Documentation Chatbot using langchain
  • Tech Stacks: LLM,Langchain,Chatgpt-3.5
  • Project Github URL: Github Link
  • Project Video URL: Demo Video

Leveraging the power of Deep Learning and GPT-3, I developed a cutting-edge AI-powered solution to streamline legal document processing. This custom chatbot acts as a virtual assistant, tailored specifically for lawyers and legal professionals, to effortlessly retrieve and analyze relevant information from complex contracts and legal documents. By integrating advanced natural language processing capabilities with the LangChain framework, this innovative model provides a seamless and intuitive interface for users to engage with and query legal documents. Through natural language interactions, users can pose questions or prompts, and the AI assistant intelligently extracts and presents the most pertinent information, saving valuable time and effort.

Project information

  • Title:Campus Guard using yolov8
  • Tech Stacks:Yolo,Streamlit,MongoDB
  • Project Github URL: Github Link

Campus guard employs YOLOv8 to detect instances of individuals without helmets or triples on campus premises. Through this innovative implementation, our system promptly notifies the respective department staff members upon detecting violations. By leveraging real-time object detection capabilities, we ensure a proactive approach to safety and compliance within the campus environment. This solution not only enhances security measures but also fosters a culture of responsibility and adherence to safety protocols among students and staff. With the ability to swiftly identify and address infractions, "Campus Guard" serves as a reliable tool in maintaining a secure and conducive learning environment.
